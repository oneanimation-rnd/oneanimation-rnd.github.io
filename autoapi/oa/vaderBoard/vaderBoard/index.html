StoryBoard Checkin Tool

Author: Enrique Caballero Follow-up: Angeline Tan Date: January 15th 2014

This is a tool for the Storyboard, the Editing and Coordination Department, its goal is to make checking in files incredibly easy, as well as helping maintain proper naming conventions and introducing safety checks.
