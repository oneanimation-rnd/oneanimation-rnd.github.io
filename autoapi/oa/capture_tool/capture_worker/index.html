 oa.capture_tool.capture_worker

Capturing must be done as a balancing act in order to capture with a decent frame rate while not overflowing the machine’s memory.

A ThreadPool is ran on a separate thread to capture pixmaps of the screen at a constant rate. For high res/long captures it’s imperative to dump those pixmaps on disc during capturing otherwise the machine’s memory will fill and make the machine unresponsive.

To solve this we use a series of Queue and Thread objects that will save all the pixmaps from separate threads.
